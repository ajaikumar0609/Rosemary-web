Three decades of nurturing young minds.

Since 1992, Rosemary Matric Hr. Sec. School has grown into one of Tirunelveli’s trusted neighbourhood schools — guided by strong values and a genuine care for every child.

Our Story

From a small beginning to a lasting legacy.

Established under the Jeya Priya Educational & Charitable Trust, Rosemary was founded on a simple belief — that a good school shapes not just careers, but character.

Since 1992, generations of students have walked the sunlit corridors of our three-storey campus in Ram Nagar, beginning each day with the school choir and morning assembly. The school sits in a quiet, verdant corner of Tirunelveli, set away from the bustle of town — a calm space where young minds can grow. Our library holds nearly 10,000 books, our labs are fully equipped for Physics, Chemistry and Biology, and our EduCamp smart classrooms bring lessons to life with interactive technology.

Our growth has always been measured not in numbers alone, but in the confident, courteous and capable young people who leave our gates ready for the world.

  • Founded 1992 · English-medium, co-educational
  • Matriculation & Higher Secondary education
  • Guided by the Jeya Priya Educational & Charitable Trust
What guides us

Mission, vision & values.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment for every child's academic, social and emotional growth — developing responsible, well-rounded citizens through quality education, personalised attention and strong community engagement.

Our Vision

Creating enlightened individuals — thoughtful, principled and capable people who carry their values and learning into a rapidly changing world.

Our Values

Knowledge, character, courtesy and service — lived daily through morning assembly, the school choir, hands-on learning and a culture of mutual respect within the Rosemary family.

Our Motto

“Bless us to be a Blessing.”

A reminder that the purpose of learning is not only to grow ourselves, but to become a source of good for our families, our community and the world around us.

From the Principal

A message from our Principal.

“Education not only imparts knowledge, it shapes character, instils values, and nurtures confidence, discipline and responsibility.”

We are deeply committed to an environment where every pupil strives for academic excellence whilst developing into a courteous, thoughtful and responsible individual. The most meaningful progress is achieved when school and home work in close partnership.

I warmly invite you to visit our campus, meet our teachers, and experience the Rosemary spirit for yourself.

Mrs. C. Indumathy
