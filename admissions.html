Scholarships & concessions

Sibling concession of 10% on tuition for the second child, and merit support for board-exam toppers. Fee concessions are also considered for single-parent families — speak to the office in confidence.

Transport (optional)

Safe, punctual bus service across Tirunelveli. ₹8,400–₹13,200 per year depending on distance from campus; route list and stops are available at the office.

What's not included

Uniforms, textbooks and notebooks are billed at cost at the start of the year (approximately ₹3,000–₹5,500 by class). There are no capitation or donation charges of any kind.

Good to know

Questions families ask.

When does the admission season begin?

We accept enquiries throughout the year. The main admission season opens in February–March for the academic year beginning in June. Early enquiry is recommended as seats fill quickly, especially for Pre-KG, LKG and Class XI. Contact the office on +91 462 2530837 or WhatsApp us for current seat availability.

Which classes can my child join?

We offer classes from Pre-KG (age 3+) through Higher Secondary (Class XII). The school follows the Tamil Nadu Matriculation Board curriculum up to Class X, and the Tamil Nadu State Board for Classes XI–XII. Entry into Class XI is into one of our two science groups: Group I (Biology) or Group II (Computer Science).

Is there an entrance test?

For most classes, admission is based on a friendly interaction with the child and a review of previous academic records — not a formal examination. For transfer students, we review the last class mark sheet and conduct a brief assessment to ensure the child settles well. The office will advise based on the class applied for.

How are fees paid — and are there concessions?

Tuition is payable in three term instalments (June, September and January) at the school office. A 10% sibling concession applies on tuition for the second child, and merit and hardship concessions are considered case by case. There are no capitation or donation charges of any kind.

What is the medium of instruction?

Rosemary is an English-medium, co-educational school. Tamil is offered as a compulsory language subject from Class I onwards. All science, mathematics and social science subjects are taught in English through the EduCamp smart-class system.

What facilities are available for new students?

Students benefit from EduCamp smart classrooms, a fully equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology laboratory, a computerised Linguistic Lab, a library with nearly 10,000 books, a NextGen Learning Hub, the school choir, and safe transport to and from the campus in Ram Nagar.

Is school transport available?

Yes. The school provides safe and comfortable transportation covering major areas of Tirunelveli. As our campus is situated in a quiet, secluded part of Ram Nagar, the transport service ensures punctual and reliable access for all students. Contact the school office for the current list of routes and stops.

How do I arrange a campus visit?

Simply call us on +91 462 2530837 or +91 92800 94836, send a WhatsApp message, or book a slot in the application above, and we’ll arrange a convenient time. School office hours are Monday to Saturday, 9:00 am – 4:00 pm.
