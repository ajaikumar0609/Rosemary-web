Spaces designed for real learning.

A calm, green campus equipped with the classrooms, laboratories and resources that bring lessons to life.

EduCamp

Smart Classrooms

Interactive EduCamp smart-class boards and visual wall magazines turn every lesson into a rich, engaging experience — from primary through senior secondary.

Air-conditioned

Linguistic Lab

A computerised, audio-visual language lab — air-conditioned and fully equipped — for immersive, confident English communication and listening skills.

Grade VI–XII

Computer Laboratory

Hands-on digital literacy and programming training from Grade VI onwards, building practical, future-ready skills in computing and information technology.

Physics · Chemistry · Biology

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology labs where senior students from Classes XI and XII conduct real experiments, titrations and discoveries first-hand.

~10,000 books

Library & NextGen Hub

A rich reading hall with nearly 10,000 books and a NextGen digital innovation space — "Reading maketh a full man." Students explore far beyond the standard curriculum here.

Door-to-campus

Safe Transport

The school is situated in a quiet, secluded part of Ram Nagar and provides safe, comfortable transport to ensure every student reaches campus with ease and punctuality.

A campus that cares

Safe, green and welcoming.

Our three-storey campus in Ram Nagar, Maharaja Nagar — set in a quiet, verdant corner of Tirunelveli — is built around a simple idea: children learn best when they feel calm, safe and inspired.

  • Spacious central ground for assembly, sports and events
  • Daily morning assembly and the beloved school choir
  • Clean, well-maintained classrooms and bright corridors
  • Safe school transport for students across Tirunelveli
