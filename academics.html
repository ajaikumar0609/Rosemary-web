How we teach

Learning that sticks, not just memorised.

Smart classrooms, well-equipped laboratories and a language lab let students see, do and discuss — not just read and recall.

  • EduCamp smart-class boards with interactive wall magazines
  • Hands-on practicals in fully equipped Physics, Chemistry & Biology labs
  • Computerised, audio-visual Linguistic Lab (air-conditioned) for English
  • Computer literacy from Grade VI — practical, future-ready digital skills
  • Nearly 10,000 books in the library plus the NextGen Learning Hub
  • Regular term and unit tests with parent-teacher progress feedback
